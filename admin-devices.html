How device linking works
🚗 Driver Device
Links a phone as a driver's delivery device. The device sees available orders in the driver's zones and can accept, collect and deliver orders. Stays linked until revoked.
🖥️ Order Viewer
Links a tablet or screen as a kitchen display. Shows all incoming orders in real time. Ideal for kitchen staff or front-of-house. Stays linked until revoked.
To link a device: Generate a code here → open Fetch app on the device → tap FETCH. logo 3 times on login screen → select role → enter code or scan QR.
